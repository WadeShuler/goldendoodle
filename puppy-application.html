Goldendoodle Puppy Application

Our goldendoodles are our fur babies! We want our puppies to go to only the best homes and the right fit. We check you out just like you checked us out! We also use the application to match you with a puppy with the right temperament for your household. When the puppies are born we watch each puppy and track them for several weeks. We will take pictures of each puppy and send them to you. Local families can visit the puppies at 6 weeks to make their decision. Puppies will be chosen in order of pick placement reserved when deposit is received.

 

Payment Info

By submitting this application, you are NOT being put on the waiting list or required to put down a $500 deposit. Only after approval and a deposit of $500 is received will you be added to a specific waiting list

The deposit for your goldendoodle puppy is $500 & goes towards the total amount of a puppy $2500. Waiting list / Puppy Pick placement is ordered upon date of deposit, a first come first serve basis. The deposit holds your pick placement on the waiting list.

If you have been approved by Portland Goldendoodles your deposit can be made on the Puppy Deposit page. Your "pick placement number" in the choice of a puppy in the litter. This deposit will hold your place on our reservation list in the order of deposits recieved when your application is approved.

This deposit is non refundable if, after reserving a puppy from Portland Goldendoodles, you decide not to purchase a dog from us. On the other hand, if we cannot provide a dog for your family we will gladly refund your deposit.
