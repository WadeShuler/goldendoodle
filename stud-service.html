Goldendoodle Stud Service

Falkor, their 3rd generation son is fully available for studding. He was born Feb 2015, and is a proven stud with a total of 10 litters so far! He is clear of all genetic health testing and OFA excellent certified hips with papers on hand for proof. Falkor is super agile and extremely intellegent. He has a soft, curly, 100% hypoallergenic and non-shedding coat. He produces apricot, red and cream colored coats. His mom Blitza has the hazel light eyed gene carried from her father, so Falkor produces a couple in the litter with liver noses and light eyes again depending on the female.

Klutch (Falkor's Father) is a F2 second generation Goldendoodle stud who is now 11 years old and is officially retired.
